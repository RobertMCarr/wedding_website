Parking

The venue has plenty of car parking spaces and cars may be left overnight whether you're staying on site or off, at the owners' risk for collection the next morning by 10:30am at the latest.

Accomodation

Accomodation is availble on site at Wasing Park; Each individually designed luxurious rooms that include a full english breakfast the next morning with the rest of the wedding party! Price per room is ₤159. Please find below a link to book using our promotional code 141120CARR. Online booking closes one week before our wedding, at which point bookings can be taken over the phone (01189713634 Mon-Fri 9am-5pm).

BOOK HERE

Rooms will be ready by 2pm and there will be a convenient opportunity to check in after the speeches. Guests have the option to arrive from midday and relax in the Long Stable. A member of staff will inform guests if their rooms become available earlier. Check out is 10.30am the next day. Breakfast on Sunday morning is served in the Long Stable at 9am for all resident guests. If you have special dietary requirements please note these on your bedroom booking form.

Order of the Day

We ask that guests arrive by 1:30pm however guests are welcome to arrive up to one hour before the ceremony.

2pm: Ceremony is at in the Garden Room.
2:30pm: Drinks, Canapes in the Garden Room, Photographs
4pm: Wedding Breakfast, Toast, Speeches in Castle Barn
7pm: Cake cutting, Drinks, Dancing!
9pm: Evening snacks

The Castle Barn bar closes at 11:15pm. Music finishes at 11:30pm.
For those staying the night in Wasing Park's on-site accommodation the bar in the Long Stable will be open from 11:30pm until 1.30am!
For those leaving us, the venue closes at midnight and it is requested that all guests have left the premises by this time. The venue asks that our guests arrange for taxi's to arrive around 11:45pm.

Confetti

Only real petals are permitted. We will make the appropriate confetti available to you on the day to throw if you wish, so no need to bring any. Confetti is only to be used in the Ceremony Room, Terrace or Castle Barn. Confetti bombs and cannons are not permitted by the venue.
