Directions

For full directions from the M4 or M3 go to Wasing Park Directions document. The venue entrance is on the junction of Wasing Lane & Back Lane. Enter through the white gate, drive through the park land (avoid the sheep!) turning left at the top of the drive, then immediately right for the car park.

Parking

The venue has plenty of car parking spaces and cars may be left overnight whether you're staying on site or off, at the owners' risk for collection the next morning by 10:30am at the latest.

Order of the Day

We ask that guests arrive by 1:30pm however guests are welcome to arrive up to one hour before the ceremony.

2pm: Ceremony in the Garden Room.
2:30pm: Drinks, Canapés in the Garden Room, Photographs.
4pm: Wedding Breakfast, Toast, Speeches in the Castle Barn.
7pm: Cake cutting, Drinks, Dancing!
9pm: Evening snacks.

The Castle Barn bar closes at 11:15pm. Music finishes at 11:30pm.

For those staying the night in Wasing Park's on-site accommodation, the bar in the Long Stable will be open from 11:30pm until 1.30am!

For those leaving us, the venue closes at midnight and it is requested that all guests have left the premises by this time. The venue asks that our guests arrange for taxi's to arrive around 11:45pm.

Confetti

Only real petals are permitted. We will make the appropriate confetti available to you on the day to throw if you wish, so no need to bring any. Confetti is only to be used in the Ceremony Room, Terrace or Castle Barn. Confetti bombs and cannons are not permitted by the venue.
